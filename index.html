Draw Platformer

Grand Prize Winner at HackUVA, March 2017

DrawPlatformer allows you to make your own Platformer style video game. With just a few markers and some printer paper, you can draw your own custom level. Then, just take a picture of your masterpiece, upload it to our server, and you have a playable game in under 30 seconds. We built this at HackUVA, UVA's premier 24-hour hackathon. Out of 35 competing teams, we were awarded with the Grand Prize (1st place) along with $1,000.

I made the front-end for the game browser, and used JavaScript to create the game’s functionalities, and my teammate used OpenCV + Skimage + Python to do the image processing on the backend.

Raptionary

Finalist Project at VTHacks, February 2017

Raptionary is a mobile application that gets you the lyrics of any song and provides an Urban Dictionary lookup tool that can give users definitions to any slang words or phrases used in a song. The program uses the jsoup library and provides lyrics and definitions by web crawling azlyrics.com and urbandictionary.com. The app has reached over 600 downloads. Download Raptionary for free on Google Play!

ESnap

VTHacks III, February 2016

ESnap is a mobile application that can provide a summary of an article or book page just from a screenshot or picture of a page. The program converts the image into text format using an Optical Character Recognition (OCR) API, and summarizes the text using a Natural Language Processing (NLP) API. The app has reached over 250 downloads. Download ESnap for free on Google Play!
